Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate?

As long as you are a high-schooler or middle-schooler, you can! This means that even if you graduated this school year, you may still participate.

What if I can't code?

Coding isn't as hard as many people think it is. With a little time and effort, you can become an amazing coder! You can email us for resources for additional help.

When will the hackathon be held?

The hackathon will start on July 17th. If you start working on your project beforehand, it will not be considered for judging.

Why should I do this hackathon?

You should participate in the hackathon because it will be fun. Not to mention, there will be prizes for the winner and minicontests as well!

Does it cost money to compete?

No. Participating in the hackathon will be absolutely free to everyone!

What workshops will be held?

Workshops will pertain to introductions to machine learning, competitive programming (USACO), Java, and Python.
