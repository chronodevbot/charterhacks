Theme

With everything going on with COVID-19, the topic of this hackathon is disease. This can be anything such as analyzing the spread of disease with machine learning or making an informative website on measles. Games, apps, websites, and machine learning projects are all allowed. If you have any questions about this topic (or anything else about the hackathon), you can email us at cswhackathon@gmail.com or talk with us through discord here.

Logistics and Submissions

You are given a week to complete your project in a team of 1-4 people. Placements will be determined by a rubric, and the highest placers will be treated with prizes. Submissions will be accepted at cswhackathon@gmail.com. Make your subject title "CH_Submission_{Your Team Name}." In your email, you should list your team members and a link or attachment to your project. Alongside these, your submission should contain a video (~2 mins) or paragraph (2-4 sentences) showing/explaining your project. Your code should be attached as well.

Grading

Grading will be fairly simple. You will be graded based on creativity, complexity, and the originality of your idea. The rubric is found here. Plagiarism in this contest will not be tolerated, though website templates and use of code snippets will be allowed (with given credit in the code). Your goal shouldn't be to win, but just to have fun!
